About Zau Academy

Our Rich History

Founded in 2007 by visionary educators in Kenya, Zau Academy began as a modest institution with a single classroom block, driven by the goal of providing quality education amid the transition from the 8-4-4 system. Over the years, we've expanded to a state-of-the-art campus, serving over 500 students from diverse backgrounds. Our journey includes key milestones like full CBC integration in 2019, multiple national awards for academic performance, and partnerships with local NGOs for community outreach programs. Today, we continue to evolve, incorporating digital learning tools to prepare students for global opportunities.

Our Mission and Vision

At Zau Academy, our mission is to foster brighter minds for an evolutionary future by delivering competency-based education that emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and ethical values. We envision a generation of leaders who drive positive change in Kenya and beyond. Guided by principles of inclusivity, innovation, and integrity, we create a supportive environment where every child can excel academically, socially, and emotionally. Our core values—Respect, Excellence, and Community—underpin all we do, ensuring students graduate as well-rounded individuals ready to tackle 21st-century challenges.
