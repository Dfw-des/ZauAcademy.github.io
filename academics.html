Our Comprehensive Curriculum

Zau Academy offers a well-rounded Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) that nurtures communication, critical thinking, creativity, digital literacy, and citizenship. Our project-based approach connects classroom learning to local contexts—environmental conservation projects, technology challenges, and community service initiatives.

Programs & Extracurriculars

All CBC Subjects

Interactive lessons across languages, maths, sciences and creative arts.

Clubs & Societies

Robotics, environmental club, drama and entrepreneurship.

Sports

Athletics, football, netball and swimming with county-level participation.

Our Staff

Our educators are TSC-registered professionals committed to continuous training and supportive student-centred learning. Non-teaching staff ensure wellbeing and campus safety.
