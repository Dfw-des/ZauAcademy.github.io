Academics at Zau Academy

Our Comprehensive Curriculum

Zau Academy proudly offers a fully integrated Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), seamlessly transitioned from the traditional 8-4-4 system to focus on practical skills and learner-centered approaches. Our curriculum aligns with Kenya's national standards, emphasizing seven core competencies: communication, critical thinking, creativity, digital literacy, learning to learn, self-efficacy, and citizenship. From early years to senior levels, students engage in project-based learning, such as environmental conservation initiatives tied to local Kenyan ecosystems, ensuring they gain real-world applicability alongside academic rigor.

Programs and Extracurriculars

We provide a wide array of programs designed to holistically develop our students:

  • All CBC Subjects: Core areas like English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Creative Arts, delivered through interactive methods including tech-integrated lessons and field trips to Kenyan historical sites.
  • Extra-Curricular Activities: Enriching options such as debate clubs, music ensembles, and entrepreneurship workshops, fostering leadership and teamwork skills essential for future careers.
  • Clubs and Societies: From environmental clubs promoting sustainability in line with Kenya's green agenda to robotics societies exploring AI and innovation, our groups encourage passion-driven pursuits.
  • Sports: A robust program including athletics, football, netball, and swimming, with teams competing at county and national levels to build physical fitness, discipline, and school spirit.

Our Dedicated Staff

Our team comprises highly qualified professionals, including TSC-registered teachers with advanced degrees in education and specialized fields. Supported by non-teaching staff like skilled chefs ensuring nutritious meals and diligent cleaners maintaining a hygienic environment, we prioritize a safe, nurturing space. Ongoing professional development, such as workshops on CBC implementation, keeps our staff at the forefront of educational best practices.
