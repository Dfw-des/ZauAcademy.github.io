Our Rich History

Zau Academy was founded in 2007 by visionary educators committed to delivering excellent, inclusive education. Starting from a single classroom block, we have grown into a thriving campus serving hundreds of learners. Major milestones include full CBC integration, strong national exam results, and partnerships with NGOs for community development.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to nurture curious, capable, and compassionate learners through a competency-based curriculum and a values-driven environment. We envision graduates who contribute positively to Kenya and the wider world.

  • Respect: We cultivate dignity and empathy.
  • Excellence: We pursue high academic and personal standards.
  • Community: We encourage service and collaboration.
